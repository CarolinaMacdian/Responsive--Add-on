Why Vila Real?

Vila Real, a "Wonderful Kingdom!"

With over seven hundred years of existence, the city was once known as the "royal court of Trás-os-Montes" due to the high number of manors bearing coats of arms and family crests, attesting the presence of noble figures that established in the city by influence of the Marquis of Vila Real, the most powerful aristocratic house in Portugal, during the 16th and 17th centuries, after the Dukes of Braganza and the Dukes of Aveiro. Many of these family crests are still visible today in the manors spread throughout the old city and in the Carreira Garden.

Café Concerto- Maus Hábitos, Vila Real

With the central structure and musical program of Maus Hábitos, the curatorship and artistic program of Saco Azul and the Mediterranean menu and signature cocktail menu of Vícios à Mesa Porto, the Café-Concerto Maus Hábitos at Teatro Municipal de Vila Real, integrates a gallery and restaurant space with a stage, presenting itself as a new agent of cultural and artistic action in the northern interior of the country, in a decentralizing logic of creation and circulation of arts and culture.

Address:

Café-Concerto Alameda de Grasse, Edifício Teatro 5000-703 Vila Real

What I like about it

The space is really cute and the food is to die for.
